Customer Experience Transformation for a Multi-Platform Digital Asset Payment Infrastructure Provider

How aCXend consolidated a fragmented, multi-CRM support ecosystem into a single unified workspace for a leading digital asset payment infrastructure provider.

60% Less platform switching by CSRs
40% Faster average response times
30% Reduction in ticket handling time
99% SLA compliance across support ops
25% Improvement in CSAT scores
The Problem

Fragmented Systems Were Slowing Every Support Case.

A leading digital asset payment infrastructure provider delivers the critical technology that powers deposits, withdrawals, and fund transfers across a diverse ecosystem of cryptocurrency and digital asset platforms — enabling its business customers to offer secure, seamless payment experiences while maintaining operational reliability and regulatory readiness.

As the organization expanded its customer portfolio, each business customer operated within its own technology ecosystem — different CRM platforms, ticketing solutions, communication channels, and support workflows. Because support spanned multiple customer environments, Customer Service Representatives had to navigate numerous systems simultaneously just to access information and manage cases.

This fragmented landscape created real operational complexity: representatives spent valuable time switching between platforms to gather information, leading to longer handling times, inconsistent customer experiences, and reduced efficiency. As transaction volumes grew, delayed responses and inconsistent support began contributing to dissatisfaction and increasing the risk of client churn.

Supporting customers across multiple disconnected CRM environments meant representatives were spending as much time navigating systems as resolving issues — the organization needed a scalable customer experience strategy to keep pace with a competitive digital asset ecosystem.
The Approach

A Unified Support Workspace, Powered by Intercom.

aCXend initiated a comprehensive Customer Experience Transformation Program, beginning with an extensive discovery phase — root cause analysis, customer journey mapping, ticket lifecycle analysis, workflow diagnostics, stakeholder interviews, and a full technology landscape evaluation — to pinpoint what was really driving service inefficiencies.

The assessment showed the primary driver wasn't workforce capacity — it was the fragmented support ecosystem itself. Leveraging its expertise in customer experience technology and Intercom implementation, aCXend designed and implemented an integration strategy that transformed Intercom into a unified support workspace, using API-driven integrations to consolidate customer records, conversation histories, tickets, and account information from multiple platforms into a single interface.

The transformation was built around several strategic improvements:

  • A unified customer view gives representatives consolidated profiles, interaction histories, and case information from multiple platforms in one place, without switching systems.
  • Intelligent ticket routing and workflow automation categorize, prioritize, and assign inquiries by issue type, urgency, and business impact, reducing manual administrative effort.
  • Real-time SLA monitoring dashboards give leaders visibility into response times, ticket volumes, backlog trends, and escalation rates across every client environment.

aCXend also established a centralized knowledge management framework and standardized operating procedures and governance to simplify future client onboarding, alongside a continuous improvement framework that folds performance analytics into regular business reviews for ongoing optimization.

The Outcome

From Fragmented Support to a Strategic CX Capability.

By centralizing customer engagement into a single operational platform, Customer Service Representatives worked more efficiently, responded more quickly, and delivered a more consistent experience regardless of the underlying CRM environment.

60% reduction in platform switching by Customer Service Representatives
40% faster average response times
30% reduction in average ticket handling time
99% SLA compliance across support operations
25% improvement in customer satisfaction scores
50% faster onboarding of new Customer Service Representatives
Improved service consistency across multiple client environments
Reduced client churn associated with delayed support responses

Through operational diagnostics, process redesign, platform consolidation, API-driven integration, and workflow automation, aCXend helped the organization turn customer support from a fragmented operational challenge into a strategic capability — strengthening loyalty, improving retention, and positioning the business to support the rapidly growing global digital asset ecosystem with confidence.
