The Problem

As content volumes rapidly expanded, the organization struggled to maintain consistent, accurate annotation across large-scale video datasets. Every frame required precise classification aligned with strict trust & safety, talent protection, and governance standards.

  • Increasing content backlog and scaling limitations
  • Risk of inconsistent labeling and missed violations
  • Quality degradation at higher volumes
  • Difficulty maintaining reviewer consistency
  • Growing demand for high-quality AI training data
Incorrect annotations didn’t just impact operations — they affected AI performance, compliance, brand safety, and talent protection.

The Approach

  • 1
    Workforce certification and structured training aligned to strict moderation and annotation standards
  • 2
    Frame-by-frame video annotation to ensure high precision labeling for AI-ready datasets
  • 3
    Multi-layer quality review: annotation → QA validation → expert escalation → continuous audits
  • 4
    Real-time quality analytics tracking accuracy, performance, and SLA adherence
  • 5
    Scalable operations model enabling growth without compromising quality

The Outcome

aCXend transformed content moderation into a highly governed, scalable, and audit-ready operation — achieving a rare outcome in large-scale data labeling: 100% validated annotation accuracy at scale.
