Scaling Customer Support Through AI-Driven Deflection and Proactive Customer Experience Optimization

How aCXend transformed a rapidly growing digital bank's support operation through AI-powered automation, self-service, and proactive CX optimization.

30% Fewer Support Tickets
Faster Issue Resolution
AI Support Automation
Higher Self-Service Adoption
Scalable Support Operations
The Problem

Scaling customer support during rapid growth without sacrificing experience.

A rapidly growing digital bank faced increasing customer inquiries as adoption accelerated.

Growing ticket volumes threatened response times and customer satisfaction.

The organization needed a scalable solution that reduced demand instead of simply adding headcount.

Analysis revealed that 30% of all support tickets came from one recurring issue that could be automated.
The Approach

Turning support data into business intelligence through proactive optimization.

aCXend stabilized support operations before analyzing ticket trends, customer journeys, and root causes.

The team partnered with customer experience, product, and engineering teams to implement AI and self-service improvements.

The solution centered on three initiatives:

  • Deploy AI-powered support for repetitive inquiries.
  • Enhance self-service journeys and knowledge resources.
  • Continuously monitor support trends to drive ongoing optimization.

This created a sustainable support model that continuously improves over time.

The Outcome

Building a smarter customer support model for sustainable growth.

AI-driven deflection reduced demand while improving customer experience and operational efficiency.

30% reduction in support ticket volume.
Reduced repetitive inquiries.
Faster response times.
Higher customer satisfaction.
Greater AI self-service adoption.
Lower agent workload.
Improved scalability.
Better cross-functional collaboration.

The engagement transformed customer support into a strategic capability powered by data, automation, and continuous improvement.
